About Us:

Your Gateway to Educational Opportunities in Cambodia!

At Gathering Scholarships, our commitment is to empower Cambodian students by providing a comprehensive and accessible platform for discovering scholarship opportunities. We understand the transformative power of education, and our mission is to bridge the gap between ambitious Cambodian learners and the scholarships that can shape their futures.

Remember, each scholarship has its own eligibility criteria and application process, so its important to read through all the details carefully. Good luck with your scholarship dream!
